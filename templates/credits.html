About the Drone Detection System

Our Drone Detection System uses advanced computer vision and machine learning techniques to identify drones in real-time. By processing video frames, the system can detect flying drones, classify objects, and alert the user. It is designed for security, surveillance, and safety applications, ensuring that unauthorized drone activities are quickly detected and reported.
